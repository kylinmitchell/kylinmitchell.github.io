Elon's Endeavors

There are wide walks of people around this vast world, each with a purpose and a life to fulfill. Those who chose to live their lives by making a difference in the world are the true heroes.

Elon Musk is the founder of Space X, an American spacecraft manufacturer with a goal of colonizing mars; a large and world-changing task to take on. He is the CEO/owner of Tesla, Neuralink, and OpenAI.

Through these advanced technologies, Elon introduced self-driving cars and paved the way for Artificial Intelligence to make an impact on the world. Without his efforts, the world would not be as advanced as it is today.
